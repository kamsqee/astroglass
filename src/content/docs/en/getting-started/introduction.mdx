AstroGlass is a premium Astro theme designed for startups and creators who want to stand out. It features a unique **Glassmorphism** design system built on top of Tailwind CSS v4.

## Why AstroGlass?

- **Premium Design**: 6 complete theme variants with depth and blur effects.
- **Hybrid Performance**: Statically generated content with server-side capabilities.
- **Developer Experience**: Type-safe content collections and easy configuration.
- **Full i18n**: Built-in support for 7 languages, easily extensible.

## What's Included?

The template comes with 6 fully designed themes, each providing 12+ page sections (Hero, About, Features, Pricing, Testimonials, FAQ, CTA, Contact, Header, Footer, plus new Landing Page sections), a dedicated Portfolio Page, a custom 404 page, plus a blog system and full documentation.
